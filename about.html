About Me

Design starts with noticing.

I am a graphic designer and web design student who enjoys creating digital experiences with personality, structure, and intention. I like when a project feels clear, but also carries small details that make people stop, look closer, and remember it.

I see design as a way to organize ideas, emotions, and visual signals into something people can understand and connect with.

How I think

Every project starts with an idea.

For me, design is not only about making something look finished. It is about asking what the project is trying to say, who it is speaking to, and what feeling should stay with the person after seeing it.

I like working with visual structure, typography, color, and interaction because each one can change how a message is perceived. Sometimes the smallest detail becomes the thing that gives a project its voice.
